About

MovieMate was created by Adam Winders for his Bachelor of Software & Data Engineering Capstone project.

The goal behind MovieMate was to create an interactive chatbot that would recommend movies based on preferences such as: favorite directors, actors, and genres.



The Creator



Adam Winders

Creator, Founder, CEO, COO, CFO, CTO, CMO


Adam started the Bachelor of Software & Data Engineering (BSSDE) program at Bellarmine University in May 2022.
Throughout the past year, he has gained experience in numerous languages such as: Java, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, SQL, Python.
Additionally, he has completed courses in: Machine Learning, Database Management Systems, Application Development in Visual Languages.
MovieMate is his first solo dev project.
